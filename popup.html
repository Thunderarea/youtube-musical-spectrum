YouTube Musical Spectrum

YouTube Musical Spectrum is a Chrome extension that offers audio visualization on your YouTube page with nice musical notes. It allows you to see waterfall of spectrogram nicely.

If you want to change the settings, click the icon on the top left corner of your YouTube page to access the menu (don't forget to reload any YouTube pages that have been opened before installation), or if it is invisible, press Ctrl+Alt+H. Subsequent click to icon will toggle the menu's visibility. On the other hand, subsequent Ctrl+Alt+H key will toggle the menu's visibility and the icon's visibility. You can also toggle the visualizer's visibility by pressing Ctrl+Alt+G.
